Our Philosophy

They say it takes a village to raise a child, and we believe the same for getting in shape. Nobody is born knowing how to get in shape, and that's where we come in. It is our goal to give our members not only access to equipment, but also access to the information they need to become fit.

Our Services

Expert Coaching

Becoming a member gives you bi-weekly access to one of our professional fitness trainers. Learn what you can do at the gym and at home to reach your goals.

Quality Equipment

While a poor craftsman may blame their tools, having quality equipment sure can make a difference. Here at Gymbos, we make sure to get high performing equipment. Equipment is also cleaned and maintained regularly to make sure that things actually work when you are here.

The Gymbo App

Fitness doesn't just happen at the gym, it also happens at home. Use our mobile app to help you reach your goals by making an excercise plan, a meal plan, and better keep in touch with your coach.
